Meet Our Team



ASHWANI GUPTA
MD

Menstrual hygiene is an integral part of ensuring better sanitation in rural villages. Construction of toilets in households and schools is integral to menstrual hygiene. Under the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, we are encouraging safe menstrual hygiene practices. Skill development and setting up of sanitary napkin dispensers and incinerators in schools and public toilets is a prime component of the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan.

Women across India grow up remaining unaware of the real reasons for menstruation and the importance of menstrual hygiene. The taboo surrounding menstruation remains a part of their growing up and continues with their daughters. Hence, the lack of awareness is carried forward via generations in India.
